05 · Principles Squint, silence, restraint. do / don't · at frame scale
Do
  • Center the wordmark, the claim, the focal artifact, the closer.
  • Fire one italic serif word per frame, inside one accent pill.
  • Keep the ink line and the flat hard shadow on every lifted surface.
  • Let cream paper breathe — sparse frames read 55–75% empty.
  • Vary the anchor; use the ledger or the ribbon to break a run.
  • Express gravity through ink outline + flat shadow.
Don't
  • Don't italicize the display sans — italic comes from Instrument Serif.
  • Don't fire two accent pills at full strength on the same frame.
  • Don't park a kicker chip in the top-left of every frame.
  • Don't render a treatment as nav + hero + features — the frame is a plate.
  • Don't use soft blur on chrome. Blur is for dimensional artifacts only.
  • Don't fabricate metrics — hold numerals as — figure — until the script.
