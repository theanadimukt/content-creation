# Skill: thumbnail-brief

## Purpose
Rewrite or strengthen the ## Thumbnail brief section in an existing script.md.
Use when the brief generated by write-script needs iteration or a full redesign.

## Input required
- Path to episode's script.md

## Output
Update the ## Thumbnail brief section in the existing script.md in place.
Do not touch any other section.

## Brief fields to output
- **Main text:** 5 words max, punchy, high contrast — placed left side
- **Sub text:** optional, smaller font, right side, max 6 words
- **Emotion:** what my face should convey if I appear (e.g. "surprised", "pointing left", "serious")
- **Background:** simple suggestion (solid colour, blurred desk, dark gradient)
- **Palette:** 3 hex values — one dominant, one accent, one text colour

## Style rules
- Clean, developer-adjacent aesthetic
- High contrast text over simple backgrounds
- Avoid clickbait that the video does not pay off
- Thumbnail text must be readable at 120px wide (mobile feed size)
- Do not use: busy backgrounds, more than 2 font sizes
- Generate 2 variants, let user pick
